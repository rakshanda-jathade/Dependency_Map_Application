The task is to identify related keywords for a given context word. For example, if "Force" is given as a keyword word you have to select respective context words which as per your knowledge you can conclude as appropriate pre-requisite topics for the given keyword like "Acceleration,Mass,Velocity ,etc" for the keyword "Force".

Also, if you do not wish to contribute for certain keyword ,you can skip using the "Skip Button" .If you want to add more keywords you can contribute using the "Add Button" .Similarly ,for deleting a particular context words use the "Delete Button".Your contribution will be recorded along with your identity in our DataBase after you click the "Submit Button" for a particular Keyword and will be observed in the Graph section (above on the heading bar as Node).You can visualise a user based graph at the end for your reference where you can check your interpretation as contributed in a form of "Linked Graph" where visualization is differentiated as keyword and context word as per your contribution.Kindly give us friendly feedback at the end of the contribution.Your contribution is valuable for the expansion of our free friendly knowledge base community .
